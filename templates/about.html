🤖 About Our System

Welcome to the P-HMR (Personalized Healthcare & Medicine Recommendation) system — your AI-powered companion for smarter and faster healthcare insights.

🌟 What We Do

  • 🔍 Predict diseases based on symptoms using AI/ML models
  • 💊 Recommend medications, diets, precautions, and workouts
  • 📈 Show disease stages and progression confidence
  • 🩺 Help users book appointments with doctors
  • 🤖 Provide real-time chatbot assistance for common health queries

🎯 Our Goal

To make personalized healthcare accessible, fast, and reliable using the power of technology.

🧑‍💻 Who We Are

This system was designed and developed as a major academic project for healthcare innovation, aiming to bridge the gap between symptoms and the right medical help.
